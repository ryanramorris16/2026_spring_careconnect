# CareConnect Account Deletion Request

To request deletion of your CareConnect account and all associated data, 
please email the CareConnect team at:

**careconnect.support@gmail.com**

Include the following in your request:
- Full name
- Email address associated with your account
- Reason for deletion (optional)

Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 30 days 
of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email 
once the deletion is complete.

Data deleted includes:
- Account credentials
- Patient and caregiver profiles
- Care tasks and health records
- Subscription and billing records
- Notification history
